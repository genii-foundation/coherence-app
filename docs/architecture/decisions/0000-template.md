# Decision record title

Status: Proposed

Date: YYYY-MM-DD

Owners: Name or role

Related questions: OTQ identifier

Related roadmap phase: Phase identifier

## Decision

State the chosen behavior precisely.

## Context

Describe the problem, constraints, and why a decision is required now.

## Evidence

Link experiments, exports, official documentation, threat models, user research, and scientific review.

## Options considered

List each credible option and its consequences.

## Rationale

Explain why the chosen option best satisfies the evidence and constitutional principles.

## Consequences

Describe benefits, costs, risks, migrations, and work that becomes unnecessary.

## Verification

List tests and operational checks that prove the decision remains implemented.

## Revisit conditions

Name the evidence, platform change, scale, incident, or product change that should reopen this decision.
