# Thesis source map

Research checked on July 10, 2026.

This document maps the Coherence product direction to the primary manuscripts in the sister Coherence Thesis repository. It separates the manuscripts' product vision from scientific claims that require independent validation.

## Primary product source

[Volume III, The Providence Imperative](https://github.com/providence-collective/coherence-thesis/blob/main/sources/manuscripts/coherence-thesis-vol3-the-providence-imperative.md#L443-L475) is the main source for the biometric product vision.

Relevant sections establish these ideas:

1. Lines 443 to 475 distinguish individual presence from relational presencing and recommend beginning with immediate value in gatherings rather than accumulated currency.
2. Lines 517 to 605 define evidence tiers and four proposed sensing streams.
3. Lines 617 to 649 describe late integration, reflective questions, and major safety risks.
4. Lines 845 to 851 require comparison with each participant's own baseline.
5. Lines 929 to 983 catalogue measurement, justice, performance, surveillance, and capture failures.
6. Lines 1035 to 1163 require voluntary use, local processing, participant ownership, open methods, and complete exit.
7. Lines 1255 to 1283 stage biometric feedback as a later Providence layer that requires clinical validation.
8. Lines 1303 to 1337 identify gathering feedback and a private personal companion as distinct products.
9. Lines 1394 to 1438 say that cited research supports correlates and mechanisms, not a validated Currency of Presence.

## Relational and scientific boundary

[Volume II, Wielding Intelligence](https://github.com/providence-collective/coherence-thesis/blob/main/sources/manuscripts/coherence-thesis-vol2-wielding-intelligence.md#L241-L283) supplies the relational foundation and the strongest skepticism.

Relevant sections establish these ideas:

1. Lines 241 to 283 frame coherence as the capacity to remain intelligently related under stress.
2. Lines 381 to 423 describe the layered Providence vision and surveillance constraints.
3. Lines 601 to 631 explain why physiology is promising while naming confounds, gaming, contemplative distortion, physiological diversity, and scaling as unsolved.
4. Lines 633 to 645 state that biometric coordination is not the current Providence MVP and remains a research question.

## Constitutional product constraints

[Volume IV, Architecting Providence](https://github.com/providence-collective/coherence-thesis/blob/main/sources/manuscripts/coherence-thesis-vol4-architecting-providence.md#L775-L845) defines the larger Providence application's constitutional posture.

Relevant sections establish these ideas:

1. Lines 775 to 845 cover sovereign identity, bounded attestations, contextual trust, and recovery problems.
2. Lines 857 to 899 permit AI sensemaking but forbid AI from determining trustworthiness or making binding decisions.
3. Lines 911 to 978 define the first Providence application and reject engagement optimization, advertising, operator owned profiles, and opaque AI.
4. Lines 1529 to 1549 require measurement to serve dignity and never rank, punish, or exclude people.

This creates an important product boundary. Coherence is a focused measurement and reflection instrument that may later serve Providence. It is not the whole Providence application, a trust oracle, or a physiological reputation system.

[Volume VII, Presencing Genius](https://github.com/providence-collective/coherence-thesis/blob/main/sources/manuscripts/coherence-thesis-vol7-presencing-genius.md#L119-L187) provides the experiential design principle.

Relevant sections establish these ideas:

1. Lines 119 to 155 treat presence as a trainable interpersonal practice.
2. Lines 177 to 187 say external instruments must serve internal development and remain empirically testable.
3. Lines 317 to 389 place mentors, cohorts, and embodied gatherings ahead of solitary technology.

## Product interpretation

The architecture follows six conclusions from this source body:

1. Begin with bounded, explicit measurement sessions.
2. Compare each person with their own context aware baseline.
3. Present trajectories, quality, uncertainty, and reflective prompts rather than a universal score.
4. Default to reflection after a session. Visible feedback can create performance and alter the observed state.
5. Keep raw physiology private and participant controlled.
6. Treat group synchrony as an exploratory research result until confounds, null models, and independent validation support stronger language.

## Scientific status

The following labels report the manuscript's own classifications. They are not independent scientific verification.

1. HRV and autonomic regulation are called established. This does not make HRV a measure of presence, authenticity, wisdom, or trust.
2. Vocal biomarkers and interpersonal physiological synchrony are called emerging. They belong in exploratory features with visible uncertainty.
3. Pulse morphology and endocrine inference are called experimental. They remain outside initial product claims.
4. A portable coherence currency and decentralized biometric trust are called speculative. They do not belong in the initial product.
5. Collective intelligence is presented as contested.

The manuscript corpus contains no concrete EEG, fNIRS, brainwave, or neurofeedback modality. The thesis is currently an autonomic and relational sensing vision. Any neurofeedback program must come from separate scientific research and its own capability, consent, quality, and validation work.
